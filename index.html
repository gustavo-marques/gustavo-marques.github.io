Physical Oceanographer

I am a Project Scientist at the National Center for Atmospheric Research working in the Oceanographic Section of the Climate & Global Dynamics Laboratory. I serve as the science liaison for the Ocean Model Working Group of CESM. I lead the development of the Modular Ocean Model version 6 (MOM6) within the Community Earth System Model (CESM) framework.

My research interestes include:

  • Ocean model development;
  • The role of the ocean in the climate system;
  • Ice/ocean interactions and their effects on the climate system;
  • Coastal ocean dynamics.
